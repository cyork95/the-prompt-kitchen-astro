AI image generation is especially powerful for design visualization — it lets you explore what a concept looks like rendered and lit before committing to a 3D model or physical prototype. The key is translating the sketch's core idea into descriptive language, then layering in render quality and lighting cues.

## Example Prompt — Architectural Concept

```
A refined concept for a futuristic architectural structure inspired by a sketch. 
A modern, flowing, curved glass and steel building with incorporated greenery on 
terraces, shot at twilight with glowing interior lights, urban skyline in the 
background. Photorealistic, detailed texture, cinematic lighting. --ar 16:9 --v 6.0
```

## The sketch-to-render translation process

**Step 1 — Describe the sketch's core idea**  
What's the defining form, material, or gesture? "Curved glass and steel" / "flowing organic shape" / "brutalist concrete cantilever"

**Step 2 — Add the render context**  
What conditions show it best? Time of day, weather, season, surrounding environment.

**Step 3 — Specify render quality**  
Photography vs. architectural visualization vs. concept art — each reads differently:

| Style | Prompt terms |
|---|---|
| Photorealistic render | "photorealistic, shot with [camera], cinematic lighting" |
| Architectural visualization | "architectural visualization, white model, studio lighting" |
| Concept art | "concept art, detailed illustration, dramatic lighting" |
| Blueprint / technical | "technical drawing, orthographic, clean lines, blueprint style" |

## Prompts for other design disciplines

**Interior design:**
```
A refined interior design concept for a minimalist living room with floor-to-ceiling 
windows, warm oak flooring, cream linen sofa, single statement pendant light, 
late afternoon light. Interior design visualization, photorealistic. --ar 16:9
```

**Product design:**
```
A refined industrial design concept for a minimal desk lamp with a brushed aluminum 
arm and matte white shade, on a light wood desk, studio lighting, product 
visualization quality, clean background. --ar 4:5
```
