**Most pricing pages answer "what does it cost?" but not "why is this safe to buy?"** This recipe builds the copy and layout layer that handles the second question — directly beneath the button where hesitation actually lives.

## The Recipe

```
Act as a world-class conversion rate optimization (CRO) expert and behavioral psychologist. I want to design or audit a digital product/SaaS pricing page to drastically maximize signups, increase average order value (AOV), and crush buyer hesitation at the exact moment of purchase.

My offer details and target audience: [INSERT PRODUCT & AUDIENCE]

Please develop a comprehensive UX, layout, and copy framework detailing:
- The Hesitation Killers: Write the exact micro-copy, trust badges, FAQ positioning, and risk-reversal guarantees needed directly beneath the primary call-to-action buttons.
- Tier Layout Strategy: Explain how to visually distinguish the "Most Popular" or highest-leverage tier to trigger choice architecture and anchor bias seamlessly.
- Explicit Feature Framing: Show me how to translate flat feature lists into direct, outcome-driven benefits (e.g., instead of "API Access," use "Automate your workflows instantly with API connectivity").
- Frictionless Checkout Copy: Provide copy ideas for micro-notes that reduce payment anxiety (e.g., "Cancel anytime in 1 click," "No credit card required for trial").
```

## Feature-to-benefit translation

| Feature copy (weak) | Benefit copy (strong) |
|---|---|
| "API Access" | "Automate your workflows instantly with API connectivity" |
| "5 User Seats" | "Bring your whole team — no extra logins" |
| "99.9% Uptime SLA" | "Your product stays live when it matters most" |
| "Priority Support" | "Get a human response in under 2 hours" |
| "Custom Branding" | "Send it with your name on it" |

## The hesitation stack under the CTA button

This is the highest-leverage real estate on your pricing page. In order from most to least impactful:

1. **Risk reversal** — "Cancel anytime. No questions asked."
2. **Social proof** — "Trusted by 2,400+ teams"
3. **Payment security** — "Secured by Stripe. Your card details never touch our servers."
4. **FAQ link** — "Questions? See our FAQ →"

All of this fits in three lines beneath the button. Don't skip it.
