Travel planning is one of AI's strongest areas. It doesn't just give you a generic itinerary — when prompted well it can match activities to your actual energy level, find the right type of stay, and surface things you wouldn't find in a TripAdvisor top 10.

## The Recipe

```
I'm planning a weekend getaway to [destination or general area — city, region, type of place].

About the trip:
- Travel party: [solo / couple / friends / family with kids]
- Vibe: [cozy and slow / active and outdoorsy / food-focused / culture and history / mix]
- Budget: [budget / mid-range / I don't mind spending]
- Dates: [specific or just "a weekend in summer / fall / etc."]

Give me:
1. 2–3 accommodation options that match the vibe (with what makes each one different)
2. A loose Saturday itinerary — morning, afternoon, evening
3. A Sunday plan that's lower effort (I'll be tired)
4. One thing in the area most visitors miss
5. One thing that sounds good in theory but to skip in practice
```

## Variations

**Local escape version** — don't know where to go yet:
```
I want to get out of [your city] for a weekend. I want somewhere within [X hours] 
drive that feels genuinely different from city life. I'm looking for [vibe]. 
Give me 3 options with a one-paragraph pitch for each.
```

**Activity-first version** — you know what you want to do:
```
I want a weekend that's mainly about [hiking / wine tasting / surfing / skiing / etc.] 
near [region]. Where should I base myself and what does a good 2-day version of 
this look like?
```

## Getting the most out of it

**Use a model with web search** (Perplexity, Gemini, ChatGPT with Search) for real accommodation and current attraction info. A model without web access will give you solid itinerary structure but won't have live availability or recent reviews.

**Follow up on specifics** — once you have an itinerary you like, drill into individual items: *"Tell me more about the Sunday morning option — what's the best time to go and is it suitable for someone who's a moderate hiker?"*

## 🔁 Leftover Remixes

**🌶️ Spicy:** *"I want to do something I've never done before. Design me a weekend that's slightly outside my comfort zone but not terrifying. I'll tell you a bit about myself: [2–3 sentences about what you normally do]."*

**🧊 Mild:** *"I just want to actually relax for two days. No packed schedule. Design me a weekend where I do as little as possible but still feel like I went somewhere worth going."*

**💰 Budget:** *"Plan me a weekend in [destination] on roughly [budget] total including accommodation. Tell me what to skip to stay in budget without ruining the trip."*
