**The hook is the only part of your content that competes.** Everything after the first line only gets read if the first line wins. This recipe generates 12 options across the four psychological triggers that actually stop scrolls — so you have variants to test, not just one to guess with.

## The Recipe

```
Act as an elite conversion copywriter and social media growth strategist. I need a matrix of high-performance, scroll-stopping hooks to lead my upcoming content asset.

My core topic/insight is: [INSERT INSIGHT/TOPIC]
My target platform is: [e.g., TikTok, X, LinkedIn, YouTube Short]
My target audience is: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]

Please generate 12 distinct hooks divided into 4 psychological categories, completely tailored to my topic:
1. The Pattern Interrupt (Challenging a universally accepted truth or common advice).
2. The Social Proof / Authority (Leading with undeniable metrics, data points, or hard transformations).
3. The Cost of Inaction (Highlighting a painful mistake or hidden loss the reader is currently experiencing).
4. The Curiosity Gap (Presenting a compelling outcome while holding back the mechanism, making it impossible not to keep reading/watching).

Keep them punchy, formatted natively for the platform, and free of cheesy, over-used buzzwords like "Secret," "Hacks," or "Game-changer."
```

## The four hook categories

| Category | Mechanism | Example pattern |
|---|---|---|
| Pattern Interrupt | Contradicts an assumption — forces reappraisal | "Everything you've been told about X is wrong" |
| Social Proof / Authority | Leads with hard data — credibility before claim | "I [did X] in [timeframe]. Here's the exact system." |
| Cost of Inaction | Names the loss — activates loss aversion | "While you're [doing X], you're [losing Y]" |
| Curiosity Gap | Shows the destination, hides the route | "The one thing keeping you from [outcome]? It's not [obvious answer]." |

## The platform formatting rule

A hook that works on LinkedIn doesn't automatically work on TikTok. The recipe asks you to specify the platform — the AI will adjust line length, capitalization style, and pacing accordingly. Keep that field specific.
