Product photography prompts follow a tighter formula than portrait or landscape work: the product is the subject, everything else is context. Surface, lighting, background treatment, and focus are the four variables that separate a product shot from a snapshot.

## Example Prompt — Minimalist Electronics

```
A professional product photograph of a sleek, matte black wireless pair of earbuds 
in their open charging case, placed on a light grey textured concrete surface, 
with a subtle background bokeh of a modern desk. Sharp focus, clean lines, 
natural daylight, 8k resolution, shot with a Canon EOS R5. --ar 1:1 --v 6.0
```

## The product photography formula

| Element | Options | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Surface** | Concrete, marble, wood grain, white seamless, fabric | Sets the brand tone — industrial, luxury, natural, clean |
| **Lighting** | Natural daylight, soft box, rim lighting, hard shadow | Natural = organic; soft box = commercial clean; hard shadow = editorial |
| **Background treatment** | Bokeh blur, gradient fade, solid white, contextual scene | Bokeh keeps focus on product; white = e-commerce standard |
| **Focus spec** | "Sharp focus", "macro detail", "tack sharp" | Prevents soft/dreamy rendering on detail shots |
| **Aspect ratio** | `1:1` for e-commerce, `16:9` for banner/hero, `4:5` for Instagram | Match output to where the image will be used |

## Style variations for different brand positions

**Luxury / minimalist:**
```
[Product] on a brushed white marble surface, single stem flower beside it, 
soft diffused window light, editorial style, clean negative space. --ar 4:5
```

**Lifestyle / in-use:**
```
[Product] being held by a hand with natural nails, casual home setting, 
warm afternoon light through a window, bokeh background, candid feel. --ar 4:5
```

**Technical / e-commerce:**
```
[Product] on pure white seamless background, studio lighting from three angles, 
no shadows, sharp throughout, standard product photography. --ar 1:1
```
