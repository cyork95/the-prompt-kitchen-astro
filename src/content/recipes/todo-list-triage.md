A long to-do list isn't a productivity system — it's a source of decision fatigue. This recipe runs your raw list through an Eisenhower Matrix plus a Velocity Score (Effort vs. Impact) to turn chaos into a clear daily agenda. 

**Note:** This is a two-step prompt. You'll send the recipe first, the model will confirm and ask for your list, then you paste it in.

## The Recipe

```
I have a massive, overwhelming to-do list that is causing decision fatigue. Act as a master organizer and guide me through triage.

Ask me to paste my raw list. Once I do, process it using a clear framework (like the Eisenhower Matrix combined with a "Velocity Score" — Effort vs. Impact).

Your output should filter my chaos into:
- The "One Thing" (The single domino task that makes everything else easier or unnecessary).
- The "Quick Wins" (Tasks taking under 5 minutes to clear mental space).
- The "Deep Work" block (1-2 major tasks requiring high cognitive load).
- The "Delegate/Drop" pile.

Give me the step-by-step instructions on how we will do this together.
```

Then paste your list when the model asks.

## The Velocity Score

The model combines two dimensions to rank your tasks:

| Dimension | Question |
|---|---|
| Impact | If this is done, how much easier does everything else get? |
| Effort | How much cognitive energy does this actually cost? |

High impact + low effort = do it first. High effort + low impact = drop or defer.

## The domino task

The "One Thing" is the task that, once done, makes other tasks on your list easier or unnecessary. It's rarely the most urgent item — it's the most *unlocking* one. The model will look for it explicitly rather than just sorting by deadline or urgency.

## What to paste

Dump everything — half-formed ideas, leftover items from last week, things you've been avoiding, vague notes. Don't clean it up first. The messier the input, the more useful the triage output.

## 🔁 Leftover Remixes

**🌶️ Spicy:** *"Now take the Deep Work block item and break it into a 25-minute Pomodoro sequence with specific sub-tasks for each session."*

**🧊 Mild:** *"Just find the One Thing from my list. One sentence on what it is and why it unlocks everything else."*

**💰 Budget:** *"Look at my list and tell me the 3 things I should do today and the 3 things I should stop pretending I'm going to do."*
