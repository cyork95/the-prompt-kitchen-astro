**Your weekly production cycle starts here.** Don't just pick a topic — build an angle matrix that gives you a contrarian take, a case study angle, and a step-by-step breakdown, plus visual concepts ready to hand to an image AI.

## The Recipe

```
Act as a master content strategist and creative director. I need to kick off my weekly production cycle by transforming rough ideas into concrete content concepts with high-visual potential for [INSERT PLATFORMS, e.g., YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn].

My core theme/topic for this week is: [INSERT TOPIC/THEME HERE]

Please run the following creative protocol to build my weekly "Brain Trust":
1. The Angle Matrix: Generate 3 distinct angles for this topic based on user psychology:
   - The Contrarian (Challenging a common industry belief).
   - The Case Study (A deep-dive transformation or breakdown).
   - The Step-by-Step (A frictionless action plan).
2. Thumbnail & Cover Concepting: For each angle, design a Midjourney/Flux prompt optimized for a high-converting visual thumbnail or header. Use clean cinematic lighting, striking depth of field, and specific style cues (e.g., minimalist studio portrait, neon tech aesthetic) to make it stand out.
3. The Platform Split: Briefly outline how this single theme will be adapted into one long-form asset and two short-form spin-offs.

Present the angles and image prompts clearly so I can select the winning concept.
```

## What you get out of Day 1

| Output | Use it for |
|---|---|
| 3 distinct angles | Pick one, bank the other two for future weeks |
| Midjourney/Flux prompts | Thumbnail creation before you even start filming |
| Platform split outline | Pre-planned Day 5 atomization work |

## Day 1 → Day 2 handoff

Take the angle you choose and paste it directly into the Day 2 prompt. The whole workflow chains — each day feeds the next.
