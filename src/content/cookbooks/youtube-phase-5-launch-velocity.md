**The first 24–48 hours determine whether YouTube buries or amplifies your video.** Early engagement signals (watch time, comments, shares) are the algorithm's green light. This phase manufactures that momentum.

## The Recipe

```
Act as a digital marketing manager and YouTube community growth specialist. My video has just gone live. I need an aggressive, low-friction "24-Hour Launch Protocol" to boost initial velocity, drive external traffic, and maximize early engagement metrics.

My published video is about: [INSERT VIDEO DESCRIPTION]

Please design a step-by-step launch execution checklist covering:
- The Pinned Comment Strategy: Draft a high-engagement pinned comment designed to spark an active discussion in the comments section (give me 2 options: a thought-provoking question or an exclusive resource link).
- Community Tab Asset: Write a compelling text + image poll post for my YouTube Community Tab that naturally leads viewers back to this new video.
- The Shorts/Reels Repurposing Blueprint: Identify the 2 most high-impact, emotional, or value-dense 45-second segments from this video topic, and rewrite them into standalone vertical video micro-scripts optimized for driving traffic to the main long-form video.
```

## The three launch assets

| Asset | Goal | When to post |
|---|---|---|
| Pinned comment | Starts comment activity, boosts engagement signal | Within 5 minutes of going live |
| Community tab post | Alerts existing subscribers, drives first-hour views | Same day as upload |
| Shorts/Reels (×2) | External traffic and algorithmic cross-pollination | Day 1 and Day 3 after upload |

## The two pinned comment options

The prompt generates both — choose based on your goal:
- **Thought-provoking question:** Best for growing comments and watch time (algorithm loves active threads)
- **Exclusive resource link:** Best for driving email list signups or community joins

## Phase 5 → Phase 6 handoff

Let the video run for 7 days before touching Phase 6. You need real data before the analytics triage is useful.
