**Role: The Researcher.** Objective: validate real market demand before finalizing development. A waitlist with 200 email signups is stronger evidence than any market research report.

## The Recipe

```
Act as an expert user researcher and growth marketer. I am building a new micro-SaaS / digital product and want to launch a pre-order or waitlist landing page to gauge real market demand before finalizing development.

My product idea is: [INSERT IDEA]
My target audience is: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]

Please design a comprehensive landing page framework and pre-launch blueprint, including:
1. The High-Converting Hero Section: Write a punchy, benefit-driven headline, a sub-headline that specifies the exact mechanism of the tool, and a compelling Call to Action (CTA).
2. The "FOMO"/Scarcity Lever: Recommend a sustainable strategy to incentivize early signups (e.g., lifetime discount, early-bird pricing tiers, beta access tokens).
3. The 3-Email Nurture Sequence: Draft three concise, automated emails to send to waitlist signups over 2 weeks to maintain high intent, share behind-the-scenes building progress, and prep them for launch day.

Keep the copy entirely free of corporate jargon and tailored to indie hacker aesthetics (clean, direct, and human).
```

## What the Researcher delivers

| Output | What it proves |
|---|---|
| Hero Section | Whether your value prop is clear enough to earn a click |
| Scarcity Lever | Whether early adopters find the incentive compelling |
| 3-Email Sequence | Whether you can maintain intent across 2 weeks without a product |

## The validation signal to watch

If the waitlist page converts below 20% of visitors, the headline isn't working — not the product. Run Phase 1 again with a different angle before building anything.
