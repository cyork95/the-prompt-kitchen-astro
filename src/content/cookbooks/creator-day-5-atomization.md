**One pillar. Multiple platforms. Zero laziness.** This recipe doesn't just repost — it transforms your finished piece into content that feels native to each channel.

## The Recipe

```
Act as a master distribution strategist and growth marketer. My main content pillar for the week is complete and polished. Now, I need to "atomize" this single asset to feed my entire ecosystem without making the content feel repetitive or lazy.

The completed pillar asset is: [PASTE FINAL SCRIPT, ARTICLE, OR TRANSCRIPT]
My target distribution channels are: [INSERT CHOSEN CHANNELS, e.g., X threads, LinkedIn posts, newsletter, Short-form reels]

Transform this core pillar into the following native assets:
- The Insight Thread/Carousel: A 5-part breakdown optimized for text platforms, featuring a high-reach hook, structural body points using bulleted layouts, and a conversion-focused ending.
- The Micro-Hook Scripts: Two 30-second vertical video scripts tracking single micro-insights from the pillar, optimized for quick pacing and high shareability.
- The Newsletter Hook: A short, compelling teaser email designed to drive clicks from my email list to the main asset.

Ensure every piece of atomized content matches the native formatting conventions and unwritten rules of its respective platform.
```

## What "native formatting" means per platform

| Platform | Format rules |
|---|---|
| X / Threads | Short punchy lines, line breaks after every sentence, hook in tweet 1 |
| LinkedIn | Slightly longer takes, professional framing, 3–5 bullets max |
| Short-form video | One insight only, hook in first 2 seconds, no setup |
| Newsletter | Teaser only — enough to earn the click, not enough to skip the article |

## Day 5 → Day 6 handoff

Once all your atomized assets are written, take them into Day 6 to build the scheduling and automation system that gets them published without manual effort.
