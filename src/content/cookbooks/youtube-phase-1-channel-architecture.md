**Most channels fail before the first video is uploaded.** Without a clear architecture — a value prop, pillar structure, and funnel — you're producing content with no distribution logic. This phase fixes that.

## The Recipe

```
Act as an elite YouTube growth strategist and channel architect. I want to build or reposition a YouTube channel to achieve maximum algorithmic authority, high subscriber conversion, and clear monetization pathways.

My channel niche/topic is: [INSERT NICHE]
My target audience is: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary monetization goal is: [e.g., AdSense, digital products, high-ticket consulting, sponsorships]

Please design a comprehensive "Channel Architecture" including:
1. The Core Value Proposition: A crisp, 1-sentence channel banner bio that instantly tells a new viewer why they must subscribe.
2. The 3 Content Pillars: Define three distinct content buckets that strike a balance between high-reach search traffic, broad algorithmic browse traffic, and deep community-building.
3. The Viewer Funnel: Map out the ideal journey a cold viewer should take, from clicking their first video to becoming a subscriber, joining an email list, or purchasing a product.
4. Competitor Differentiation: Identify how to visually and tonally stand out from the top 3 established channels in this space by exploiting their content gaps.
```

## The four outputs explained

| Output | What it gives you |
|---|---|
| Core Value Proposition | Your channel bio — 1 sentence that earns the subscribe |
| 3 Content Pillars | Search traffic + browse traffic + community — balanced |
| Viewer Funnel | The path from cold click to subscriber/buyer |
| Competitor Differentiation | The gaps the top 3 channels are leaving on the table |

## Phase 1 → Phase 2 handoff

Once you have your pillars defined, take the first video concept into Phase 2 for title and thumbnail packaging. The architecture informs the packaging — don't skip order.
